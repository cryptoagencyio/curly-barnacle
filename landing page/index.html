Creative Web & App Developer

NFT
Access

The Block (the NFT linked to your BLOCKBURST) represents a pixel. This pixel participates in a work of art that will be composed of all the pixels owned by our BLOCKBURST holders. Everyone can choose the location of their pixel in the final work. The goal is to create a unique community work forged by the owners.

Each BLOCKBURST is linked to a Non-Fungible-Token (NFT), unique in nature, constituting a work of art and thus allowing the application of tax regimes related to the latter. Check with the tax office of your place of residence to find out the amount of tax. This work will be sold at auction with passive income donated annually to the future buyer.
